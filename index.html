About Ken

Born in Calgary on December 18, 1975, Ken Staples grew up in the nearby town of Airdrie from 1980 to 1995. He returned to Calgary, where he has lived ever since.

Ken has a well-rounded work background with experience in various customer service and retail positions. Since 2018, he has been working at Amazon, demonstrating his commitment to a fast-paced environment. Prior to that, he held positions at several Calgary institutions, including Dairy Queen, Calgary Stampede Food Services, Eaton's (now defunct), Coles Bookstore, The Real Canadian Superstore, HomeSense, and Delta Airlines. This diverse experience has undoubtedly equipped him with strong customer service skills and the ability to adapt to various work environments.

Beyond customer service, Ken possesses a talent for technology. He is comfortable writing code in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which are fundamental building blocks for web development. Projects include this very website plus upcoming websites at theNeighbourhoods.com and TotallyRandomThoughts.com. Additionally, his proficiency in Microsoft Windows and Office applications showcases his well-rounded computer skills. This combination of technical and interpersonal abilities makes Ken a valuable asset in today's job market.
